**Title ideas (high CTR):**
- 7-Step Checklist to Spot & Report Fake Shopify Stores
- Fake Store / Lookalike Domain Incident Checklist (15 min)

**Goal:** collect email → upsell to Sprint.

## Checklist (7 steps)
1) Google quick scan: `"BRAND" scam`, `"BRAND" fake`, `"BRAND" site:.shop`  
2) Typos list: brand + `shop/store/sale` + TLD variants (`.com/.net/.shop/.store`)  
3) Reverse image search 2–3 hero product photos  
4) Shopify indicators check (checkout / `/checkouts/` / `cdn.shopify.com`)  
5) Capture minimal evidence (3 screenshots + timestamps)  
6) Identify infra (WHOIS/NS/CDN)  
7) File the first 2 reports (host/CDN + platform) + start a tracker
