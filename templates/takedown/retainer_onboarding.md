## What Is the Retainer?

**The Brand Clone Monitoring + Rapid Response Retainer** provides ongoing protection against clone stores, lookalike domains, and brand impersonation.

**What it is:**
- Daily monitoring for new threats
- Rapid response when incidents occur
- Evidence vault continuity
- Proactive threat hunting

**What it is NOT:**
- Guaranteed prevention (new clones can appear)
- Legal services (we refer to counsel when needed)
- 24/7 on-call (response during business hours)

## Success Metrics (What We Track)

### Operational Metrics

- **Incident detection rate:** New threats found per week
- **Response time:** Hours from detection to first action
- **Resolution time:** Hours from start to threat neutralized
- **False positive rate:** Non-threats flagged (should be <5%)

### Business Metrics

- **Incident frequency:** Trends over time
- **Threat types:** Phishing vs counterfeit vs impersonation
- **Repeat offenders:** Domains/actors that reappear
- **Cost per incident:** Retainer value divided by incidents

### Client Satisfaction

- **Response quality:** Are we addressing the right threats?
- **Communication:** Are reports clear and useful?
- **Value perception:** Is retainer worth the cost?
- **Referral potential:** Would client recommend us?

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How quickly will you detect new clone stores?

**A:** Typically within 4–24 hours, depending on visibility. Some clones are discovered quickly (active ads), others take longer (SEO-only sites).

---

### Q: Can you prevent clones from appearing?

**A:** No, but we can reduce risk through domain variant purchases, proactive monitoring, and rapid takedown when discovered.

### Q: What if a clone appears outside business hours?

**A:** We'll detect and respond within 2 hours of next business day. Enterprise tier includes priority 4h response.

---

### Q: Do you guarantee removal?

**A:** No. Takedowns depend on platform policies and evidence quality. We use best practices and follow-up persistence, but outcomes vary.

### Q: Can I cancel anytime?

**A:** After the 3-month minimum, yes with 30 days notice.

### Q: What if I need more than the included sprints?
