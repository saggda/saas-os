## Case Study Structure

### 1. Challenge (The Before)

**What was happening:**
- Brand discovered clone store/lookalike domain
- Harm being caused (revenue loss, chargebacks, brand damage)
- How long it had been ongoing
- What the client tried before (if anything)

**Key metrics (before):**
- Revenue loss per day/week
- Chargeback volume
- Customer complaints
- Traffic diversion (estimated)

---

### 2. Action (What We Did)

**Our approach:**
- Sprint scope (S1/S2/S3)
- Evidence captured (what, how much)
- Platforms contacted (Shopify, CDN, registrar, ads, search)
- Follow-up cadence (6h, 24h, 48h)

**Timeline:**
- Day 0: Intake + evidence + initial filings
- Day 1: Follow-ups + additional evidence
- Day 2: Escalation (if needed)
- Day 3: Resolution

---

### 3. Result (The After)

**What changed:**
- Domains taken down
- Ads removed
- Search results deindexed
- Customer protection restored

**Key metrics (after):**
- Time to resolution
- Revenue saved (estimated)
- Chargebacks prevented
- Brand damage mitigated

---

### 4. Proof (Evidence)

**Screenshots/images:**
- Before: Clone store screenshots
- After: "Site not found" or challenge page
- Side-by-side comparisons
- Case ID confirmations

**Testimonials (if available):**
- Client quote about the experience
- Specific outcomes mentioned
- Would recommend statement

## Anonymization Guidelines

### What to Redact
- Client's last name (unless permission given)
- Specific case IDs (use format [SP-XXXXX])
- Internal notes or sensitive details
- Other brands mentioned (if confidential)
- Revenue figures (if client requests)

### What to Keep
- First name or title (e.g., "Founder, DTC brand")
- Brand name (if permission given) or industry
- Domain names (redacted like [brand]-fake.com)
- Timeline and process details
- Results and metrics

### When to Ask Permission
- Using real brand name
- Using client's full name/title
- Sharing revenue/sales figures
- Using client photo/likeness
- Publishing on public channels

**Default:** Ask permission for anything beyond "[Industry] Brand" anonymized format.
